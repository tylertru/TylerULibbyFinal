FAQs

Q: The book I searched for didn't bring any results on the "search" page. Why?

A. While the Libby App is constantly growing its catalog of literature, sometimes the

content isn’t available from your selected library. The Libby App does extend its search

to nearby libraries when your selected choice doesn’t have the book you’re looking for.

Unfortunately, that doesn’t always fix the problem. As a result, we are always looking to

work with more libraries to help ensure the Libby App community find all their

requested literature.

 

Q. What are skip-the-line books?

A. Skip-the-line books are unique pieces of literature found on the Libby App that are noted as special by your selected library.The skip-the-line books will never have

waitlists and their copies can’t be renewed. Your library may choose to have shorter

loan periods for them, as well. Additionally, your library may limit the number of skipthe-line books you can borrow, at once. In conclusion, skip-the-line books are a way for

libraries to provide their members access to popular books, as quickly and efficiently, as

possible.

 

Q. What is the difference between the Libby App and Overdrive App?

A. The Libby and OverDrive Apps both allow for their users to loan content from their local

libraries. What makes the Libby App unique is the way it is shaped by us to most benefit

consumers of literature. For instance, the Libby App condenses all your holds from

nearby libraries into one app. Additionally, the user can use our tags feature to organize

books in their own way. Your catalog between libraries is seamlessly interconnected and

you can begin reading immediately after you borrow. We are also very proud of our

settings and accessibility options that aim to assist the user in an inclusive and friendly

manner. We believe each of these facets of the Libby App, and more, help make it

special place for all consumers of literature.

 

Q. Why can’t I download the Libby App successfully?

A. The most common issue our users encounter when installing the Libby App is not having

their device up to date. The Libby App works only on devices iOS 10.0 or later and

Android devices 5.0 or later. You may need to check your device settings for a

manufacturer update. Alternatively, your device may need more storage space to install

the app. On iOS devices the Libby App requires 19.4 MB of storage space while Android

devices require 3.3 MB

 

Q. What if I can’t finish my book by the return date?

A. The Libby team completely understands that a reader’s plans may change! Because of

this, the Libby App provides users the ability to extend their loans.

 

Q. How can I get a library card to use the Libby App?

A. While ever library has its own process to receiving a library card, most of them require

you to provide contact information before they can send you a personal library card. All

library cards are free to receive, in The United States of America. Libby also

interconnects library catalogs near you so that you don’t miss out on any great pieces of

literature!
